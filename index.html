About

I'm a Ph.D. student at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Texas at Austin, working with Prof. David Z. Pan. Previously, I received the B.S. in Microelectronics, Shanghai Jiaotong University in 2013. My research interests include physical design and design for manufacturing.

I have received Franco Cerrina Memorial Best Student Paper Award at SPIE Advanced Lithography Conference 2016, and National Scholarship at Shanghai Jiaotong University in 2012. I have interned at IMEC, Cadence, and Oracle.
